Our Legacy

Krupa Ayurveda Marma Hospital stands as a living tradition of healing, rooted in the sacred wisdom of Ayurveda, Siddha, Marma Chikitsa, Tribal Medicine, and Parambarya Treatments. Our legacy spans generations, dedicated to restoring harmony between body, mind, and spirit through natural and time-tested therapies.

Our journey began in the early 1980s under the divine guidance of Beevi Ayshumma, the first Guru of Ajmeer Vaidhyar. Known for her intuitive healing power, Ayshumma treated people from across India and even foreign visitors from France. Her compassion and mastery of traditional marma therapy became the foundation of our healing lineage. She served humanity selflessly until her peaceful passing at the age of 107.

Following her blessings, the lineage was carried forward by Kunjunj Aashan (Sreedharan Vaidhyar) of Perya, Wayanad ‚Äî a respected vaidhyan, astrologer, and visionary architect who combined the science of healing with spiritual understanding. His contributions brought new depth and structure to the Krupa Ayurveda tradition.

In the 1990s, the legacy was further strengthened by U.P. Muhammed Vaidhyar, a renowned Parambarya Marma Chikitsakan with vast knowledge in astrology, poison treatment, tribal healing, and medicinal herbs. His profound insight into natural and spiritual healing elevated the hospital‚Äôs reputation as a true center of holistic health.

Continuing the lineage, Razak Gurukkal joined as a dedicated disciple, mastering Kalari and Marma therapies and integrating the wisdom of movement and energy into the Krupa healing system.

Today, Krupa Ayurveda Marma Hospital serves patients across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Andhra Pradesh, offering authentic Ayurvedic and Marma treatments passed down through generations of healers. Our mission is to preserve and share this sacred knowledge ‚Äî helping every individual achieve balance, vitality, and inner peace through the timeless path of Ayurveda.

Ayurvedic Treatments

In Ayurveda , healing is not a battle against illness it is a return to the natural rhythm of life. Every drop of oil, every herb, every breath of steam carries the silent wisdom of centuries, whispering balance back into the body and calm into the spirit.

Here, treatment is more than a remedy; it is an act of remembrance of who we are when we are whole. Each therapy touches deeper than the skin, reaching the channels of energy that flow through every cell and thought. The body begins to listen, the mind begins to quiet, and the heart begins to bloom again in stillness.

Through Panchakarma, we cleanse what time and tension have left behind.
Through Marma Therapy, we awaken the hidden gateways of vitality.
Through the healing herbs of nature, we nourish what was weakened and restore what was lost.

Ayurveda teaches that pain is not the enemy it is a message, a whisper from within, asking for care and attention. When we honor that message, healing begins.

Here, under the wisdom of Ayurveda, we bring together ancient science and sacred touch to guide the body back into harmony, the mind into peace, and the soul into balance once more.
